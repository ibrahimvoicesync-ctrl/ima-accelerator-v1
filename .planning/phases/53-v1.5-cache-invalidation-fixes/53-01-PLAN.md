## STRIDE Threat Register

| Threat ID | Category | Component | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |
|-----------|----------|-----------|-------------|-----------------|
| T-53.01-01 | Information Disclosure | coach leaderboard staleness | mitigate | This plan itself — immediate cache bust means coach sees accurate up-to-date numbers within one request; previously masked by 60s TTL. Correctness, not confidentiality. |
| T-53.01-02 | Denial of Service | repeated coach-cache invalidation from a student spamming PATCH completed | accept | Existing `checkRateLimit(profile.id, "/api/work-sessions/update")` at 30 req/min bounds invalidation rate. | T-53.01-03 | Tampering | student targeting another coach's cache key | mitigate | `coach_id` is looked up via admin client keyed on `profile.id` (derived from `auth.getUser()`); attacker cannot inject a different coach_id through request body. |
| T-53.01-04 | Elevation of Privilege | student cross-tenant cache bust | accept | Cache-bust affects freshness, not data access. Even if an adversary could trigger a foreign coach's bust, no data is exposed — the cache is repopulated from the same RLS-scoped RPC on the next read. |
